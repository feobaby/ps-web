Isomorphic strings

[javascript]

A javascript app that checks two strings s and t, to determine if they are isomorphic. Two strings are isomorphic if the characters in "s" can be replaced to get "t". For Example: "egg" and "add" are isomorphic, "foo" and "bar" are not, “paper” and “title” are isomorphic.
