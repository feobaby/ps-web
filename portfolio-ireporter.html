About this project:

iReporter is a web application that enables any/every citizen to bring any form of corruption
to the notice of appropriate authorities and the general public, also users can report on things
that needs government intervention.

Technologies used:

  • Node.js
  • Express.js
  • Postgres
  • Heroku
  • Pivotal tracker
  • Travis CI
  • Git
  • Html/Css3
